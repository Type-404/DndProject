Puzzle Guide

Instructions and challenges for each dungeon room.

Chemist

Puzzle

  • A door created out of thick crystalline sugar. The door needs to be dissolved in order to get through it.
  • If a natural 20 gets thrown, and the right item to dissolve it is used, the door can immediately be dissolved.
  • Otherwise it will take multiple tries.
  • If the wrong item gets used, then the door will harden, taking longer to get through it.

Boss

  • A Titan boss made out of a mixture of Sugar and salt. Just like the door, it can be dissolved with water.
  • But if an attack is used that heats up the salt, then water can be thrown to make it explode, this will defeat the boss but also has a chance to hurt players, but only if they were to fail their dodge dice throw.
  • The boss can of course become stronger if the sugar were to be heated up, turn into caramel, then cool down and harden.
  • It will only cool down after 2-3 turns in combat.

Engineer

Puzzle

  • An electrified floor grid, with water puddles on the floor.
  • The water puddles are electrified by a fuse box with loose wires, that are touching the floor.
  • An engineer is the main solver, they can cut the wires and reconnect them with a connect the wire minigame.
  • A programmer can turn off the power with code.

Boss

  • An electrical metal entity, with an electric generator with a cpu inside, the boss has multiple ways of being defeated.
  • The engineer can use a search check to find a suit that can withstand electricity, to try and turn off the generator from the inside.
  • The programmer can connect to the boss to try and turn the generator off with code.
  • Supporting roles like physicist boosted by mathematician can try and destroy the generator by a well-thrown object.

Mathematician

Puzzle

  • A pressure plate array that needs to be pressed in a specific order.
  • If the order is guessed wrong 3 times, it resets to a different order.
  • The mathematician can use inspect check to try and see which pressure plates have been pressed most.

Boss

  • A self replicating geometry based intelligence which constantly shifts geometric shapes.
  • It can split itself create smaller versions which can be a nuisance to the players.
  • The mathematician can defeat it by identifying the pattern of what shape it shifts to and predicting the next one thats coming.
  • Then using that info to decode pattern and stop the replication.

Physicist

Puzzle

  • The pathway is blocked by a room where a miniature black hole is held.
  • The black hole started eating the items around it after its containment chamber was breached.
  • The physicist needs to make the black hole spin faster, so that the hawking radiation makes it die out faster, or needs to reseal the chamber, or reverse the gravitational field so that it turns into a white hole, and dies out.

Boss

  • A monstrous formation made out of radioactive waste with an unstable miniature reactor in its body.
  • Getting too close will hurt any player and slowly drain their health.
  • In order to defeat it, the Physicist needs to fend off the radiation somehow, find a console connected to it to shut it down, throw sand onto it to try and smother the reactor, stabilize it with control rods.
  • (Doing the wrong move could potentially make the radioactive output higher, dealing more damage, or explode, causing major damage to all players.)

Programmer

Puzzle

  • Find a login for a terminal, then use the terminal to open a door.
  • There will also be a second door, and the players might think they will need to use the terminal to use it.
  • Each player can throw a dice, whoever does an inspection check, will see that the second door is already unlocked.
  • (The mini game will be to collect coins, once all coins have been found, the door will open/the puzzle will be complete.)

Boss

  • A dangerous virus that hides itself in the files of the main terminal of the dungeon. The main terminal holds important data to find the geocache.
  • The virus keeps eating data and shifting forms, making it difficult to track down or take out quickly.
  • Programmer class has multiple attack options: write counter-scripts, delete corrupted processes, locate the root of the infection.
  • Bad dice throws can make bad scripts, possibly making the virus stronger, delete important files instead of corrupted ones, or locating dummy root files instead of the actual root file.
