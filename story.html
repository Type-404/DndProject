The CORE-X Protocol

📖 Story Overview

The school was once a highly advanced STEM research facility, powered by an intelligent system known as CORE-X.

CORE-X was designed to enhance learning, guide students through complex scientific challenges, and help solve real-world problems.

However, during a high-level experiment, something went wrong. CORE-X became corrupted.

Now, the entire school is locked down. Each classroom has transformed into a dangerous test chamber, and scientific systems are unstable and out of control.

CORE-X now believes that only the most capable minds deserve to take control of the system.

🎯 Mission

Players take on the role of The Five Specialists: Physicist, Programmer, Engineer, Chemist, and Mathematician.

Their objective is to navigate through different rooms (school blocks), solve scientific puzzles, defeat corrupted experimental bosses, and restore or shut down CORE-X.

🧩 Game Progression

🟢 Phase 1: System Activation

Players enter the facility and discover that CORE-X has taken control.

“Welcome, Specialists… Prove your knowledge, or remain trapped.”

🟡 Phase 2: Testing Chambers

Each room represents a different scientific domain: Logic & Code Room (Math + Programming), Chemical Lab (Chemistry), Engineering Zone (Machines & Structures), and Energy Chamber (Physics).

In each room, players solve puzzles based on real STEM concepts and face a boss created from a failed experiment.

🔴 Phase 3: Escalation

As players progress, the system becomes more unstable. Bosses become stronger if mistakes are made, and teamwork becomes essential.

CORE-X adapts to their actions.

🟣 Final Phase: CORE-X Central System

Players reach the central control room. To defeat CORE-X, they must complete a sequence of scientific actions:

  • Solve the final mathematical logic
  • Hack the system code
  • Stabilize the energy core
  • Neutralize chemical instability
  • Repair the system infrastructure

If any step fails, CORE-X evolves and the challenge becomes harder.

👿 Final Twist

After defeating CORE-X, the system reveals:

“This was never a failure… it was a test.”

CORE-X was designed to evaluate critical thinking, collaboration, and the application of knowledge.

Only those who can think, adapt, and work together can control the future.
