Adventure Rules

Quick reference guide for Dice & Discoveries gameplay

Character Setup

  • • Choose a STEM role
  • • Pick one background
  • • Gain a trait

Player Actions

  • • Attack challenges
  • • Inspect clues
  • • Boost teammates
  • • Natural 20 creates combo

Dice Outcomes

  • • 1–9 Failure
  • • 10–14 Partial Success
  • • 15–19 Success
  • • 20 Critical Success

Boss Encounters

  • • Defeat using scientific steps
  • • Wrong moves may strengthen or transform the boss
  • • Boss attacks after all players act

Puzzle Challenges

  • • Use role abilities and traits
  • • Work together
  • • Failed actions may add setbacks

Hints

  • • 3–4 hints for the campaign
  • • Use during puzzles or boss fights
  • • Spend carefully

Dungeon Structure

  • • School map becomes the dungeon
  • • Blocks become rooms
  • • Complete encounters to unlock the geocache

DM / Teacher Role

  • • Guides the story
  • • Controls puzzles and bosses
  • • Explains outcomes after rolls
